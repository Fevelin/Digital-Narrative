The Donkeyman

-"Oy, did you get any new fancy women?" Asked the captain with a raised eyebrow. -Heeeeeee-Yaaaaaahaw, Alfred Jones, the terrible asking me for a woman?! Heee-Haaaaaw!!! Miss Price the one you need! She just came today! Said cheerily the donkeyman and leaned closer to the captain. -"Listen here captain, she is a ship-in-bottle bombazine!" The captain frowns his eyebrows. He doesn't believe the donkeyman. -"Show me then!" Said the captain and the donkeyman gestured to follow him.
